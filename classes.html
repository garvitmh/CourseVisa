Course Details

This Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) course introduces you to the fundamentals of patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and industrial designs. You will learn how to protect your innovations, manage IP assets, and understand international IP laws. The course includes practical case studies to help learners connect theory with real-world application.

Certification

Upon successful completion of this course, you will receive a verified certificate from Coursiva, demonstrating your knowledge of Intellectual Property Rights. This certificate can be shared on LinkedIn and with potential employers.

Who this course is for

  • Students in law, business, or engineering who want to understand IP basics.
  • Entrepreneurs and startups looking to protect their ideas and innovations.
  • Professionals in R&D, design, or product management roles.
  • Anyone interested in learning about global IP rights and enforcement.

What you'll learn in this course:

  • Introduction to Intellectual Property Rights and their importance.
  • Patents: filing, duration, and case studies of patent disputes.
  • Trademarks and branding strategies for businesses.
  • Copyrights in creative industries: music, films, books, and software.
  • Trade secrets and industrial designs.
  • International IP laws: WIPO, TRIPS Agreement, and regional differences.
